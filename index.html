2. Visual Dashboard

This embedded dashboard is powered by Google Looker Studio. In production, it would be connected to the Excel/Google Sheet generated above.

What this dashboard shows

  • Overall IN, OUT and current stock across all zones.
  • Filters to drill down by zone, brand and material.
  • Stock levels by zone and material, and brand share.
  • Movement trends over time and a table of recent transactions.
  • Spike alerts for unusually large OUT movements.
